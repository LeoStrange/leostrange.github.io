Walka-Bot



To get started, use walkahelp, and you'll see the commands list.
Walka-Bot offers some commands to add Ben 10 content to your Discord server.
Here's some of the available commands:
-Walkamoji: Walka-Bot will send some emojis of him.
-Walkaclip: Walka-Bot will send a clip of him from the series.
-Walkaverse: Walka-Bot will send a emoji of one of his version randomly.
-Walkaquiz: Play a quiz related to Ben 10.
Also, you can add all the Walkatrout emotes to your server using just ONE COMMAND!.
