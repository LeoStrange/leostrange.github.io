Bolin-Bot



Bolin-Bot offers The Legend of Korra content for your Discord server, such as GIFs, quotes from the series, etc.
To get started, use b!help, this command will show you the featured commands and a link to my website with the complete list.
Here's the list of the featured commands:
• info: Sends a short version of the Bolin Wiki.
• clip: Sends a random Bolin clip.
• earth: Sends a random Earthbending clip.
• quiz: Starts a quiz related to The Legend of Korra.
